Our Mission

We are an event company which serves as a one stop shop for all your event needs. Our goal is to provide a unique experience for you that is tailor-made to your specific needs. Reducing stress and eliminating the unnecessary and unwanted hassle associated with event execution. We got you, let us help you create lasting memories.

Why ELD? We strive to always provide quality service, remain customer focused prioritizing your needs and we dare to be creative/innovative always pushing the envelope to create new and exciting event ideas.

Our Management Team

I got into event management because it became my escape from everyday life. The endless possibilities of what I could help create always brought about excitement. I have received my professional certificate in event management at Arthur Lok Jack Global School of Business. This course is in collaboration with George Washington University and is taught by quite a few of our own local event professionals.

I have been doing events since my early teens, first alongside my mom, helping with school events, bridal showers, concerts, and the list goes on. But it was helping plan a wedding that showed me a gap in the market, giving me the idea to start Every Little Detail Events. I wanted to create a business that could fit multiple needs, be affordable and different. I can honestly go on and on, but I’ll stop here, I can’t wait to help you make lasting memories at your dream events.
